## The Step-by-Step Playbook: From Zero to $1M/Year

### Step 1. Pick One Niche
Specificity beats universality. Pick a niche to dominate:

#### High-Frequency Markets (Best for Automation)
* **Bitcoin/Ethereum 5-minute rounds:** High frequency, clean external signals (Binance), constant inefficiencies.
* **Stock Market Daily Closes:** Individual stocks, NASDAQ, S&P 500. Clear endpoints and predictable spikes.

#### Sports Markets (Large Audience)
* *Toolkit:* Composio's API-Sports covers 2,000+ competitions.
* *The Edge:* Injury and lineup data. Odds often lag 5–10 minutes behind confirmed team sheets.
* *Categories:* NFL, NBA, Premier League, MLB, Tennis.

#### Politics & Elections (High Volume)
* **US Elections:** $500M+ volume. State-by-state, candidate chances.
* **International:** UK, France, Brazil.
* **Events:** Cabinet appointments, policy outcomes.

#### Weather & Natural Events
* **Weather Markets:** Models (NOAA, ECMWF) update faster than Polymarket odds.
* **Hurricane Tracking:** path projections, intensity forecasts.

#### Entertainment & Pop Culture
* **Awards:** Oscars, Grammys. Predictable timelines and leaks.
* **Box Office:** Opening weekend numbers.

#### Crypto & Tech
* **Regulations:** ETF approvals, SEC decisions.
* **Earnings:** Tech company revenue beats/misses.

#### Economic Indicators
* *Toolkit:* Composio's API Ninjas toolkit (interest rates, inflation figures).
* **Fed Decisions:** Basis point predictions.
* **CPI/PCE Reports:** Predictable monthly cycles.

#### Emerging Categories
* Space/Science, Health/Pandemics, Supreme Court decisions.

### Step 2. Study Market Movement
Find repeatable patterns:
* Reaction speed to external data.
* Liquidity clusters in the order book.
* Duration of inefficiencies.

### Step 3. Convert Pattern into Code
The typical architecture:
1.  **Monitor** external data (Binance, Sports APIs, Weather).
2.  **Detect** the lag (when Polymarket doesn't match reality).
3.  **Auto-enter** before the book adjusts.
4.  **Auto-exit** after probability syncs.

*Note:* Claude can now act as the development layer. You describe the logic, and Claude generates the architecture, API connections, and monitoring scripts.

### Step 4. Turn Tools into Infrastructure
What used to take weeks now takes hours. Your product lineup:
* **Bot Subscriptions:** Ready-made solutions with customizable parameters.
* **Copy-trading Dashboards:** Follow the best wallets.
* **Market Scanners:** Real-time anomaly detection.
* **Execution Engines:** High-performance systems for pros.

### Step 5. Monetize
Four revenue tiers:
1.  **Bot Subscriptions:** $200–$500/month (Mass market).
2.  **Private Tools:** $1,000–$5,000/month (Serious traders).
3.  **Automation Dashboards:** $5,000–$10,000 setup + monthly (Teams/Funds).
4.  **Institutional Solutions:** $10,000+/month (Enterprise).
