## Phase 2: Mastering Entry & Exit Filters

This is where most traders fail. You must match your trade size logic to the trader's activity level.

### 1. How to Copy Buys
Choose the method that fits your bankroll:
* **Fixed (Recommended):** Every trade is the same amount (e.g., $50). *Use this if the trader makes <10 trades per day.*
* **Portfolio:** You copy the *percentage* of the balance they use. *Use this if the trader makes 10+ trades per day to scale safely.*
* **Percentage:** You enter a specific % of the trader’s actual position size.

### 2. How to Copy Sells
* **Same % (Recommended):** If the trader sells 50% of their bag, you sell 50%. This fully replicates the professional’s exit logic and is the most balanced approach.
* **Manual:** You copy the entry but decide the exit yourself. Only for those with high market conviction.
